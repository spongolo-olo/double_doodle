Welcome to Double Doodle!

A collaborative drawing game of imagination and translation. Create unique artwork together!

How to Play:

  1. Player 1 (The Initiator): Picks a line color and draws a single continuous line without lifting their pen/finger to make an abstract starting shape.
  2. Player 2 (The Finisher): Receives the doodle and uses free strokes, colors, and brushes to turn the shape into a recognizable drawing!
Step 1 Player 1: Select a color and draw a single continuous line. Don't lift your finger!
Step 2 Player 2: Turn this shape into something recognizable!
